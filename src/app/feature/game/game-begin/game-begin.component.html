Rules

Player and AI are playing the game. From the pile of 25 matches, each player takes either 1, 2 or 3 matches on each turn. The game is over once all matches are taken. Whoever has the even amount of matches wins.

  1. Start with a pile of 25 matches.
  2. Two players take turns to play the game.
  3. On each turn, a player selects either 1, 2, or 3 matches from the pile.
  4. The selected matches are removed from the pile.
  5. The game continues until all matches are taken.
  6. The player who ends up with an even number of matches is declared the winner
